Wishing

This section is about the wishing system and will be broken up into multiple parts.

Primogems

Primosgems also known as primos is the wishing currency. You can gather primogems by doing quests, your dailies (we'll get to those later), chests, and the hangout quests.

Aquaint Fates

The fates you can use to wish on the standard banner. How to gather them you say, Primos every 160 primos = 1 aquaint fate you can wish 1 aquaint fate or 10 aquaint fates.

Intertwined Fates

The fates you can use to wish on the limited banner. Again every 160 primos for 1 intertwined fate.

Character Banner

The character banner is the first of two Limited banners. Every 90 pulls you get the highest rarity (5*) characters and every 10 pulls you get a 4* character/weapon (lowest a character can get).

Weapon Banner

The weapon banner is the other one of the limited banners. Every 80 pulls you can get a 5* weapon and every 10 pulls you get a 4* weapon/character.

Beginners Wish

This wish is part of the standard wish where you can only do 20 pulls, each are 20% off and you can only do 10 pulls (8 aquaint fates per 10 pull) you have a loqw chance to get a 5* but you are gaurenteed at least 2 4*s. you will be gaurenteed to get the character Noelle but you can also get others too.

Standard Banner

This banner has 7 5* characters in it and every single 4* in the game. this one works the same as the character wish but you can also get 5* weapons aswell.

Pity

Pity is a system you have. Every so often you can get a 5* it is random but when you get to 70 pity you have a higher chance to get a 5*, HOWEVER you can lose something called a 50/50 where you could get one of the 7 5* characters on the standard banner and then not get the character you want.

Domains

Threr are lots of domains in total there are 5 types of domains.

One time domains

These domains are the ones tha are used in the Archon quests (The main quest) or are just used to get some primos.

Weapon Ascension Domains

Weapons need to be ascended and will need a special item for the weapon. There is one weapon domain for every region and the items rotate out of the domain, however every Sunday ALL domains have all of their items you can farm for.

Tallent Domains

The tallent domains are just like the Weapon domain the tallent peices rotate and are always open on Sundays and every region has one.

Artifact Domains

There are multiple artifact domains per region each only have two types of artifacts in every domain that are mainly focused. The artifacts DON'T rotate and are open every day.

Weekly Domains

The Weekly domains you can fight weekly and the domains can only be unlocked through quests (the Main archon quests and Story quests).
